CAP 18 Begins!

Posted on Mar 3, 2014

Led by Topic Leader, DetroitLolcat, Smogon's first XY Create a Pokemon project has officially begun!

CAP is a competitive-minded project whose aim is to learn more about the metagame and what goes into a successful competitive Pokemon by building them from the ground up. The process differs from most fake-mon projects in the way that it is competitively-focused, community-driven, and focuses on a concept to fulfill which is voted on at the start of each project. New users should take some time to get used to the forum and read up on the subject. Once they're feeling comfortable, they should submit a concept! Who knows, they could have the winning one!

To read more about the CAP project's history, rules, and creations, check out the EnCAPlopedia, which is full of helpful links and references. ~darkie

SPL Farm League

Posted on Dec 23, 2013

Missed the signups for the Smogon Premier League? Sign up for the SPL Farm League!

The Farm League allows even unknown or under-the-radar players to make a splash in SPL and then be drafted into the SPL during the mid-season auction. Performing well will dramatically increase your chances of being on a SPL team for the second half of the season!

To sign up, post with your name and tier that you'll play in (XY OU, XY Ubers, XY LC, XY Doubles, BW OU, BW UU, BW RU, BW NU, DPP OU, ADV OU, GSC OU) in the sign up thread. You can sign up for the farm league at any time until it is over and then you'll be entered into the battles for the next week and onwards!

Happy battling and have a merry christmas! ~darkie

Kangaskhanite banned from OU

Posted on Dec 18, 2013

Kangaskhanite has been officially banned by the OU Tiering Council. A quick run-down of the reasoning, with more information in the linked thread, is as follows:

  1. Mega Kangaskhan, with an effective 213 base attack thanks to Parental Bond, is too powerful for even the bulkiest of OU physical walls. Damage calculations are listed in the thread.
  2. Mega Kangaskhan is nearly impossible to revenge kill outside of fast and powerful Fighting-types. Other Pokemon who outspeed are either OHKOed by a +2 Sucker Punch or too weak to break through its 105/100/100 defenses.
  3. Mega Kangaskhan severely limits teambuilding by forcing teams to run multiple obscure counters and checks.

For more information, check out the official announcement thread which details exactly why Kangaskhanite is considered Uber at this time. Please note that this quickban is merely an extension of the initial banlist and may be reconsidered as the metagame develops. ~darkie

CAP 6 Playtest Cawmodore

Posted on Nov 16, 2013

The Create-A-Pokémon community would like to show off our newest creation: Cawmodore! After 112 days chock full of discussions, submissions, and voting phases, we've come a conclusion within the past few days. Although the final stages of the process were diverted due to the release of Pokémon X and Y, we managed to persevere and complete the project. Cawmodore is a Steel / Flying Pok&eacute;mon that's designed to utilize underused moves. In our initial discussions, we decided as a community that we wanted to focus specifically on the risk and reward aspects of Belly Drum. You'll notice that we considered this move at every stage in our process, from selecting Intimidate as its competitive ability to its blazing base Speed of 118.

Now that we've completed our new Pokémon, it's time for the official CAP 6 playtest to begin! The ladder is limited to a two week period where competitive Pokémon battlers will play in a metagame consisting of BW2 Overused Pokémon plus Cawmodore. This isolated test will determine how well Cawmodore held to its original concept. Is Belly Drum viable in the fifth generation of Pokémon? Help us find out by battling on this playtest ladder whenever you get the chance. If you like to experiment and battle in a fun, creative, and combative environment in OU with an entirely new competitive Pokémon, check out Pokémon Showdown! to see what the fuss is all about.

If you're interested in the Create-A-Pokémon Project, check out the CAP forum, where we're running a variety of side projects. We still have to decide on Cawmodore's third ability and Pokédex entries, so jump in to discuss and vote. Scope out the CAP sub-site if you're looking to discover more information on CAP. As we close out the fifth generation of Pokemon creation, I'd like to personally thank all of our fans and supporters. The community is what makes this project such a massive success! Have patience with the Policy Review Committee as we re-work the project for the sixth generation. See you on the other end! ~Birkal
